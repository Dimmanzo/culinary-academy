About Us

Our Culinary Journey: Celebrating Years of Passion and Excellence

Founded in 2008 by renowned chef and culinary enthusiast, Chef Michael Johnson, Culinary Academy began as a humble cooking school with a big dream: to inspire a love for cooking and empower individuals to explore their culinary creativity.

Today, Culinary Academy stands as a beacon of culinary excellence, inspiring thousands of students each year to pursue their passion for cooking and embark on rewarding careers in the culinary arts. As we celebrate over a decade of culinary innovation and education, we remain deeply grateful to our dedicated instructors, passionate students, and loyal supporters who have made this incredible journey possible.

What You'll Learn: A Taste of What's to Come

  • Culinary Theory and Principles: Gain a deep understanding of culinary theory, ingredient sourcing, flavor profiles, and menu planning, laying the groundwork for culinary creativity and innovation.
  • Global Cuisine Exploration: Explore the diverse world of global cuisine, from classic French fare to exotic Asian delicacies, discovering new flavors, techniques, and cultural traditions along the way.
  • Specialized Culinary Skills: Whether it's baking bread, crafting artisanal pastries, or mastering the art of chocolate-making, our specialized courses offer in-depth training in a wide range of culinary disciplines.
  • Fundamental Cooking Techniques: From knife skills to sauce-making, students will master the foundational techniques that form the backbone of any great cook's repertoire.
  • Professional Development: Prepare for success in the culinary industry with courses in kitchen management, food safety, menu development, and career planning, setting the stage for a fulfilling career in the culinary arts.
